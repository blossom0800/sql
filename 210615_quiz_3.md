`SELECT EMPLOYEE_ID, FIRST_NAME, LAST_NAME, SALARY, COMMISSION_PCT,
DECODE(COMMISSION_PCT, '>0' , SALARY + SALARY * COMMISSION_PCT, SALARY) REAL_SALARY
FROM EMPLOYEES;`

>> SALARY + SALARY * COMMISSION_PCT 값이 계속 다르게 반환되어서 (SALARY만) 원인 파악 중
